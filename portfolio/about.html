AJ Chapman

Hi! I'm AJ, a first year student at the University of Oregon with plans of taking nursing prerequisites next academic year. I'm Filipino-American and have lived in both the Philippines and USA, and hope to move to Australia in the future! When I'm not doing classwork, I enjoy folk music, playing the guitar, and chatting with friends.

My understanding of HTML and CSS prior to college was limited to simple text editors on browser games' social forums, such as Transformice and Wolvden. I was driven to take a course in these languages because while I had some interest in them before, I never knew where to start. If I stay at the University of Oregon, I would like to minor in Art and Technology, and if I do transfer to a community college for nursing, I would still want to expand into programming languages as a hobby!
